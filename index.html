I Saw You Again

I saw you again,
my heart remembered — it’s you.
It forgot the days between;
it only knew your smile.

You looked at me,
and the world fell quiet.
I tried to hide my trembling joy,
but my soul was already dancing.

You make me feel
like the luckiest of all.
For a moment, time bent its knee,
and love — sweet, shy, and certain —
found me again,
in the place where prayers begin.

— Written by Caramine Blessina
